#h I.A.4.e. Simple partial seizures with psychic symptoms - illusions
These take the form of distorted perception in which objects may appear deformed. Polyoptic illusions such as monocular diplopia, distortions of size (macropsia or micropsia) or of distance may occur. Similarly, distortions of sound, including microacusia and macroacusia, may be experienced. Depersonalization, as if the person were outside his body, may occur. Altered perception of size and weight of a limb may be noted.
